B O O K I N G - P O L I C Y


  1. Refund Policy:

    2. - No refunds will be provided after the booking has been confirmed.


  2. Rescheduling Policy:

    3. - Customer are allowed to reschedule their session to another available date and time by clicking the rescheduling link provided in their confirmation email.

    - Rescheduling requests must be made at least 4 hours before the scheduling session.

    - There is no limit to the number of times a customer can reschedule their session.


  3. Deposit Forfeiture:

    4. - A 100% deposit is required to secure the booking.

    - If a customer arrrives late for their session but within 15 minutes grace period, the session will be proceed as scheduled.

    - If a customer arrives more than 15 minutes late for their session, 50% of the deposit will be forfeited and the session will be rescheduled to another date and time.


  4. Notice of Rescheduling:

    5. - Customer are required to provide notice of their intention to reschedule at least 4 hours before the scheduled session.

    - Failure to provide the required notice will result in the 50% forfeit of the deposit.


  5. Rescheduling Availability:

    6. - Rescheduling is subject to the availabilty of the studio, customer may check for availability via the rescheduling link.

    - The studio will make every effort to accomodate rescheduling requests, but availability cannot be guaranteed.


  6. Additional Items:

    7. - We require the deposit to confirm your commitment in attending the session.

    - Remaining payment on additional items such as additional person, additional time and extra prints or softcopies will only be finalized at the studio.


  7. Late Appointment Policy:

    8. - Customer are granted 15 minutes grace period for their scheduled appointment.

    - If a customer arrives late for their session but within the 15-minute grace period, the session will be proceed as scheduled.

    - If a customer arrives more than the 15-minute late for their session, 50% of the deposit will be forfeited and the session will be rescheduled to another date and time.


  8. Cancellation Policy:

    9. - Cancellations are not eligible for refunds.

    - Instead of a refund, customers may reschedule their session to another available date and time, as long as the request is made at least 4 hours before the scheduled session.
